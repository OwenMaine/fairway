ABOUT FAIRWAY RESTAURANT EQUIPMENT

Fairway Restaurant Equipment, Contracting, Inc. has been serving the restaurant industry for over 20 years. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality restaurant kitchen equipment at competitive prices. Our experienced team of professionals can assist you with every step of the process, from selecting the right equipment to designing and installing your dream kitchen. We pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service and our ability to meet the unique needs of each of our clients. Whether you are opening a new restaurant or upgrading your existing kitchen, Fairway Restaurant Equipment is here to help you succeed.

We offer a wide range of services, including equipment sales, kitchen design, equipment installation, and maintenance. We work with all types of restaurants, from small cafes to large fine-dining establishments. Our goal is to provide you with the tools and expertise you need to create a successful and efficient kitchen.
